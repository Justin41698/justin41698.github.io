About Me

I currently reside in Des Moines, Iowa, where I attend Grand View University. I am chasing a degree in Elementary Education while specializing in Special Education. I also will be getting my minor in Psychology. I also spent two years at South Dakota State University. My current hometown is Clarion, Iowa, but I also like to claim Toledo, Ohio as another home of mine, as that is where I was raised until about 15-years-old.

What I Do

If you are reading this you must have found this page because of wrestling. I am currently working every day to earn a National Championship. I took 3rd place at Nationals last year, and my team won their 7th straight Natty Title! One of my biggest passions is coaching. I offer clinics, one-on-ones, week long camps, and more. I want to help create amazing wrestlers.

Why I Do It

When I was young, I had many great coaches. They all impacted me and changed me in tremendous ways. I would not be the person I am today if it was not for them. I want to be able to impact kids that same way. I also want to help keep them out of trouble and off the streets by doing something productive with their lives! Lastly, I am doing this to spread my love for a sport that has done so much for me! Wrestling is the oldest and greatest sport in the universe, and I want other people to enjoy it as I have!
